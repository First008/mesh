# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in our
community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body
size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender
identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing othersâ€™ private information without permission

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Project developers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards and
will take appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all project spaces and in public spaces
when an individual is representing the project.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive or unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting
the project team at **security@yourdomain.com**.  
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
